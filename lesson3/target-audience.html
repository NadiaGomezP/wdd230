Target Audience

The target audience for riachuelochamber.com are for those people that love to travel (individually or as a group) around the world and take beautiful pictures of the historical monuments. In Argentina, Buenos Aires there is a place that everyone should know called La Boca, its streets, buildings, restaurants and the famous soccer stadium "La Bombonera" are places that everyone will appreciate.

Personas

Fictional Name: Alvin Jackitori
Job Type: Travel and tourism agent
Demographics: He is 34 years old and he has a master degree in business management.
Environment: He works in a travel agency company and is responsible for researching important monuments around the world to add more attractions to the agency.

Fictional Name: Jefferson Gutierritos
Job Type: Freelancer Photographer
Demographics: He is 27 years old and he has more than 10 years of experience taking pictures.
Environment: He loves to travel the world and take pictures of all tourist places. His photos are so well known that many people buy them and ask him to take photos in different places.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: The site offers tourist packages.
Scenario 2: The site shows the weather of the week in La Boca.
Scenario 3: The site has detailed information about every place including address, prices and pictures.
Scenario 4: The site provides a map of La Boca neighborhood.
Scenario 5: The site shares the history of the most important stadium of Argentina "Alberto J. Armando" or most known as "La Bombonera".
