About Us

Mission Statement

Bountiful Foods started as a small project in 2011 to help small businesses that grow organic food.
Our mission is to promote the consumption of organic food and grow in different parts of the country to improve the quality of life of the population.

Background and History

We began operating in 2011 out of a small closet. Driven by need, we have expanded to now serve all of the east cost from Los Angeles to San Diego and operate at least 50 food trucks sited in differents parts of the country. We receive and collect donated food from local grocery stores, local food producers and private individuals. We sort, organize, store and distribute more than 2,000,000 pounds of organic food each year, without charge, to the low income families living in Buzos Country. In FY22, our market received more than 10,000 orders between our local and online shop.

We have food trucks in different parts of the United States, stretching from the Los Angeles coast to San Diego.
We have been awarded on different occasions thanks to our services.

In providing supplemental food to our clients and learning about their individual needs, we have found that most face numerous obstacles and that providing supplemental food is not enough to alleviate their suffering and help them move towards healthy food. In our quest to help address these issues, we found that there are existing programs and resources available but they were not being fully utilized due to lack of accessibility, lack of awareness of the programs, or inability to navigate the multiple facets of the enrollment process. Our client services begin with an enhanced client intake process to identify any special needs or circumstances, and try to assist in meeting those needs by referrals to assistance programs and partnering with other aid organizations wherever possible as we focus on providing immediate hunger relief.
